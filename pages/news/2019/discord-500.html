Our PUBG Mobile Discord Community has surpassed 500 members! We reached this first milestone recently and couldn’t be more ecstatic! Continuing forward, every obstacle that the community and the clan faced, I did my best to overcome, but I would have NEVER reached this milestone without YOU GUYS, and when I say that, I am not only talking about the staff, but also you members.
